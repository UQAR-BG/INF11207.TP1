- targets behind firewalls, check open ports

--- Idle Scan ---
sudo nmap -Pn -sI <ZOMBIE_HOST_IP> <TARGET_HOST_IP>

--- Xmas Scan --- (TCP SYN packet with unusual flags turned on)
sudo nmap -sX <TARGET_HOST_IP>

--- FIN Scan ---
sudo nmap -sF <TARGET_HOST_IP>


- packet crafting

--- Malformed data --- (knocking over systems, evading IDS)
sudo hping3 <TARGET_HOST_IP> -U -S -s 55355 -d 8080

--- Ethernet packet generator ---
sudo packeth


- banner grabbing

(get info on a potential service)
telnet <TARGET_HOST_IP> <TARGET_PORT> (p. ex. 22)

nc -v <TARGET_DOMAIN> 80
HEAD / HTTP/1.1
Host: <TARGET_DOMAIN>

(check mail service)
dig mx <TARGET_DOMAIN>
nc -v <TARGET_MAIL_DOMAIN> 25

(for protocols with encryption)
openssl s_client -connect <TARGET_HOST_IP>:443
HEAD / HTTP/1.1
Host: <TARGET_DOMAIN>


- enumeration

whois <TARGET_DOMAIN>
dig @<DOMAIN_SERVER> <TARGET_DOMAIN> axfr

dig @<DOMAIN_SERVER> version.bind txt chaos

net view \\<TARGET_HOST_IP> (windows)


- SNMP (Simple Network Management Protocol)

--- SNMP walk --- (system info)
snmpwalk -v 1 -c public <TARGET_HOST_IP>
